About EDUGRADER

EduGrader is an intelligent subjective answer evaluation system designed to revolutionize the way exams and assessments are graded. Our cutting-edge technology uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to automatically evaluate and compare subjective answers with reference answers, ensuring accuracy and fairness.

Keyword Matching

Identifies key terms and concepts to measure the relevance of answers.

Synonym Recognition

Comprehends variations in phrasing to evaluate the true meaning behind responses.

Typo Correction

Automatically fixes minor errors to focus on the content rather than the form.

Shortform Expansion

Recognizes and replaces abbreviations for a more complete analysis.

Mathematical Evaluation

Includes the ability to evaluate and validate simple mathematical expressions for correctness and accuracy.

Multi-format Support

Handles inputs in various formats such as images and files for seamless integration.
